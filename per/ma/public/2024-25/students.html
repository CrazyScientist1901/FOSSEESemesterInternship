House Activities

The house activities like recitation,story narration, solo dance were conducted to develop confidence in students. Themes like compassion, acceptance, resilience were taken for story narration and recitation to align with the vision. Activities like sharing affirmations and Geeta Saar were also conducted. Quiz club was introduced to cover a few aspects of intellectual curiosity. Activities like softboard decoration with theme Indian culture and celebrations like Janmashtami, Dusshera and Holi helped students to know their culture.
